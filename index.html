Luigi Asprino

Ph.D.

I'm a Research Assistant at the Semantic Technology Laboratory (STLab) (Institute of Cognitive Sciences and Technologies (ISTC) of the National Research Council (CNR)) in Rome, Italy. I received a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Bologna. My research interests and activities mainly include: knowledge engineering, empirical semantics, semantic web and common sense knowledge.
