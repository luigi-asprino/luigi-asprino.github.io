About me

I'm a Research Assistant at the Semantic Technology Laboratory (STLab) (Institute of Cognitive Sciences and Technologies (ISTC) of the National Research Council (CNR)) in Rome, Italy.

I received a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Bologna under the supervision of Prof. Paolo Ciancarini and Dr. Valentina Presutti

My research interests and activities mainly include: knowledge engineering, ontology design, semantic web and common sense knowledge.
